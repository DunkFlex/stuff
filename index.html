Tatsuki-san (達樹)


One morning in a house in Japan, Naru Bloggs opens a gift from her brother, Daniel Bogtrotter, and Naru knows their lives will never be the same again.
Whilst trying to rebuild her life, Naru witnesses a crime that leads her to question a new relationship. She becomes obsessed with enigmatic stranger Tatsuki Haru.
What is his connection to Daniel, and why has he turned up now?
Naru's behaviour becomes increasingly erratic as she struggles to unravel the truth and the significance of a ribbed torch, all whilst battling to cope with amnesia.
Every day, Naru gets closer to the truth. And the closer she gets, the more improbable it seems.

Plot

Taken place in the 90s Japan, we follow a group of gifted hackers. The group formed "ElseI" consist of 3 individuals- Naru Takayami, Tatsuki Haru and Miyami Takeshi. All of them shares a desire to have a better life.

>

The story will detail the experiences of the 3 individuals in their attempt to rob several banks or goverment bodies via cyber trespassing. The drama that unveils and the detailed works of a hacker shines through each second of suspense and thrills this show has to offer.
